About Us

8,000 square feet of quality hardware, trusted brands, and hometown service in the heart of Franklinville, NY.

Our Story

Franklinville Hardware originally opened its doors in 2021 as Franklinville True Value Hardware, bringing a full-service local hardware store back to the Franklinville, New York community. From day one, our goal has been simple: give our neighbors a place where they can find what they need, get honest advice, and feel right at home.

Today, our 8,000 square foot store is packed with an extensive variety of merchandise, from everyday essentials to specialty items. We carry the brand-name products that professionals and homeowners trust, and we back them up with the kind of knowledgeable, personal service that big box stores just can't match.
