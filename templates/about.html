About Us

Get to know the people and story behind Franklinville Hardware.

Our Story

Franklinville Hardware has been a cornerstone of the Franklinville, New York community. What started as a small shop with a simple goal — to provide quality hardware and honest service — has grown into the trusted resource that neighbors depend on for all their project needs.

Through the years, our commitment has never wavered. We believe that a hardware store should be more than just a place to buy supplies. It should be a place where you can get advice, share ideas, and feel like part of a community.

Franklinville, NY

Our Mission & Values

Our mission is simple: to be the hardware store our community deserves — one that offers fair prices, quality products, and the kind of personal service that big box stores just can't match.

Part of the Community

Franklinville isn't just where we do business — it's home. We're proud to support local events, sponsor community initiatives, and be a place where folks can come for help with anything from a leaky faucet to a full renovation.

We believe in the power of a strong community, and we're grateful to be a part of this one. Thank you for choosing to shop local.
